Alex's Poem


A Sad Little Frog

A little frog on a lilypad in a pond.

He laid awake singing a sad song.

He missed his toad friend who moved away,

and hoped he could see him one more day.

Little frog singing a sad song


all day long.


But the fog cleared and the frog jumped with joy

Toad was here, his favorite boy!

They hugged and he smiled with glee.

Toad made him so happy.
